Michy's Favorite Keycaps

Play me to try a new set of good vibes!


1. Chinese Love Story: A Work of Art

First of all, these keycaps look amazing. The art was what drew me to this set. I really like Chinese-themed art, and the color scheme makes it all the more outstanding. These keycaps are also PBT material, which is really durable. The OEM profile is the standard key profile, which I actually prefer the most. However, these keycaps are are particularly thoccy. The spacebar is also taller and thicker than standard OEM spacebars, giving it a nice, deep sound. My cup of tea!
Link to Amazon page (not sponsored)

2. GMK Milkyway Demon Girl: Enticingly Adorable

I really like this set of keycaps, but unfortunately, I wasn't able to get my hands on it. GMK keycaps are one of the higher-end brands of keycaps, so even just the base kit went for $90. This set is gorgeous though, with a color complement akin to the GMK Olivia++ keycap set! I also really like the novelties included :)

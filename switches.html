Michy's Favorite Switches

Play me for a short burst of happy music!


1. KTT Peaches - Linear Sweets with a Buttery Press

At first, I was a bit dubious of these switches, as they were sold by a line that I hadn't heard of before. I decided to purchase them off of Dangkeebs and was pleasantly surprised by how soft they feel. I thought Gateron milky yellows were premium budget linears, but I think these switches definitely topped them!
Short sound test!

2. Gateron Milky Yellows - The Classic Budget Premium

I had just mentioned these switches above, but these switches truly do set themselves apart from some more mainstream linears, like Cherry Reds. Milky Yellows are a variant of the Gateron Yellow switches; what makes Milky Yellows different is their milky-styled housing. The overall experience is a lot smoother of a keypress.
Short sound test!
